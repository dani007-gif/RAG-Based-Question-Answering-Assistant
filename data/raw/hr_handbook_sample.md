# HR Handbook — Sample Document

## Vacation Policy

Employees are entitled to 25 days of paid vacation per year, accrued monthly.
Unused vacation days (up to a maximum of 5) may be carried over to the following year.
Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval.
Remote workers must be available during core hours (10:00–16:00 local time).
All remote work arrangements must be reviewed annually.

## Security Policy

All employees must use two-factor authentication for company systems.
Passwords must be at least 16 characters and rotated every 90 days.
Employees must report security incidents within 24 hours to security@company.com.

## Equipment & Onboarding

New employees should submit an equipment request form within their first week.
Standard equipment includes a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse.
Additional peripherals can be requested with manager approval.
